ℹ️ How to generate the correct CSV
  1. Go to YATA Faction Attacks and click Create new attacks reports.
  2. Select the exact Start Time of the war.
  3. Press the Live button (Note: YATA cannot create reports longer than 7 days).
  4. Once generated, click the Eye Icon to view the report.
  5. In the top right corner, click Export > Attacks. Upload that downloaded CSV here!
